Service Packages Overview
This document describes the three core service packages built on top of the cloud-org-infra platform.

Each package is designed to deliver clear business value: saving time, reducing costs, and removing repetitive technical work.

Package 1: Infrastructure Deployment & Automation
What it is
A fully automated, production-ready Azure infrastructure deployment.

What the client gets
Secure cloud foundation (networking, security, monitoring)
Repeatable, idempotent deployments
Clean naming and tagging standards
Centralized logging and diagnostics
CI/CD-ready infrastructure
Ideal for
Startups
Small and medium businesses
Teams that want to move fast without breaking things
Package 2: AI Assistant (RAG-based)
What it is
A custom AI assistant connected to the client’s data and workflows.

What the client gets
AI that understands their internal data
Faster answers, fewer manual searches
Reduced support and operational effort
Controlled, secure access to information
Ideal for
Customer support teams
Operations teams
Knowledge-heavy businesses
Package 3: Automation Robots for Repetitive Work
What it is
Automation that turns hours of repetitive work into seconds.

What the client gets
Automated workflows across tools and systems
Email-to-data extraction
API-based integrations
Reliable, maintainable automation logic
Ideal for
Operations
E-commerce
Finance & admin-heavy workflows
Core Value Across All Packages
Save time
Reduce costs
Remove boring, repetitive work
Increase reliability and consistency
Quietly remove technical chaos from daily operations
Summary
These packages are designed to work independently or together.

At the core, cloud-org-infra provides the stable platform. On top of it, automation and AI deliver real, measurable business value.
