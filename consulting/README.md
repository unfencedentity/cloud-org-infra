# Consulting Methodology

This folder documents the internal consulting approach used to assess, stabilize, and standardize cloud foundations.

The methodology is designed to guide conversations and engagements from initial discovery to a clear, low-risk implementation decision.

It focuses on clarity, predictability, and risk reduction rather than tools or specific technologies.

---

## Structure

The process is intentionally sequential:

1. **Discovery**  
   Understand how the cloud environment evolved, how standardized it is, and how safe it is to change.

2. **Reflect and Frame**  
   Synthesize discovery insights into a clear, shared problem statement.

3. **Bridge**  
   Connect the framed problem to a logical engagement direction without pitching solutions.

4. **Pitch (Core Cloud Foundation)**  
   A short, outcome-focused explanation of the foundation engagement.

5. **Next Step: Assessment**  
   Introduce a small, paid assessment to replace assumptions with facts.

6. **Objection Handling**  
   Address common concerns through validation and reframing.

7. **Assessment Overview**  
   Define the purpose, scope, input, and output of the initial technical assessment.

8. **Assessment Technical Focus**  
   Outline the structural areas reviewed during the assessment without performing delivery work.

---

## Notes

This methodology is intended as an internal reference.

It prioritizes consistency, calm execution, and decision support over persuasion or speed.
